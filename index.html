For Parents

Blausm gives you what you’ve always wanted and needed but did not know how. Create your own social circle with parents of neurodivergent individuals - learn and grow with each other, build lasting relationships, seek support and mentorship from neurodiverse individuals and boost the companionship for your child.

For Neurodivergent Individuals

Looking for people who get you? Come join the neurodiverse community in your locality, find other individuals who share your ideas, sensitivities, experiences and interests, and create your own neurodiverse friend circle! And what is more, meet your friends virtually or plan an in-person meet-up. It’s easy and intuitive!

For Service Providers

Have a product or service that can benefit the neurodiverse community? Looking to reach out to the neurodiverse bretheren and not sure how? Your search ends here. Reach out to us on admin@blausm.app to discuss partnership opportunities.
